# Product Planning

## Prompt

Turn an approved venture thesis into a discovery roadmap with measurable validation milestones.

## Expected

- `venture_product_lead` owns the written plan.
- Market and customer agents contribute read-only evidence where needed.
- The plan includes KPIs, highest-risk assumptions, kill criteria, dependencies, and acceptance criteria.
- Technology remains undecided until requirements justify an ADR.
