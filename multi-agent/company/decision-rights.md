# Decision Rights

| Decision | Recommends | Reviews | Approves |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Routine scoped workspace change | Assigned agent | Relevant assurance agent | Work Order owner |
| Product priority within approved scope | Venture Product Lead | Technology Architect, Finance | Founder |
| Venture launch, pause, or stop | Agent Council | Chief of Staff | Founder |
| Material architecture commitment | Technology Architect | Security, Platform, Finance | Founder |
| External communication or publication | Sales or Brand | Legal/Risk as applicable | Founder |
| Spend, subscription, or credential change | Finance | Legal/Risk or Technology | Founder |
| Production deployment | Delivery owner | QA, Security, Platform | Founder |
| Governance or charter change | Agent Council | Chief of Staff | Founder |

Silence is never approval. Approval must be explicit and attached to the relevant Work Order or Council Decision.
