Welcome to the Living Loom

The Sky Loom produces drifting Glim, but only capture fills your Reservoir. Catch a mote, then place a producer and a capturer to discover the pattern.

Pointer: click motes and place on the field. Keyboard: Tab to a Weftling, use Left/Right to target a mote and Enter to catch. During placement, use arrows and Enter. Escape cancels.
