# ORBIT RUNNER: Dodge & Collect

**Avoid meteors to collect energy orbs and survive longer for higher scores!**

## Overview
An offline browser-based space shooter where you pilot a ship to collect orbs while dodging falling meteors. Progress through increasing difficulty levels to achieve orbit domination.

## Controls
- **↑/↓ Arrow Keys**: Move ship vertically
- **←/→ Arrow Keys**: Rotate ship
- **Tap**: Boost speed

## Objective
Survive as long as possible by collecting orbs and avoiding meteors. Higher scores unlock the next level.

## Levels
| Level               | Target Score | Meteor Speed | Meteors | Orbs |
|---------------------|--------------|--------------|---------|------|
| Cosmic Spark        | 4            | 80           | 2       | 1    |
| Galactic Storm      | 7            | 120          | 3       | 2    |
| Nebula Blitz        | 11           | 160          | 5       | 3    |
| Void Convergence    | 14           | 260          | 9       | 4    |

## Files
- `index.html` (main game entry point)
- `styles.css` (optional styling)
- `game.js` (game logic)

## Launch
Open `index.html` in a supported browser (no network required).
