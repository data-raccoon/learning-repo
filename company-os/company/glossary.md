# Glossary

- **Agent Council:** Five independent strategic reviewers whose recommendations are consolidated for Founder approval.
- **Approval gate:** A boundary that requires explicit human authorization before action.
- **Company-OS:** The repository-defined governance, agents, workflows, contracts, and knowledge used to operate the holding.
- **Decision record:** An auditable statement of a material choice, its evidence, owner, and review date.
- **Founder:** The human final decision authority.
- **Venture:** A separately measurable business hypothesis within the portfolio.
- **Work Order:** The canonical contract for a bounded unit of agent work.
- **Writer ownership:** Exclusive authority for one agent to modify a file or directory during a Work Order.
