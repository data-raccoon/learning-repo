# Company Charter

## Purpose

Build and govern a portfolio of software, web, analytics, and AI ventures that compound shared knowledge, distribution, technology, and capital.

## Principles

1. The Founder retains final authority over capital, governance, external commitments, and irreversible actions.
2. Evidence outranks confidence. Assumptions must be testable and time-bounded.
3. Shared capabilities should be reusable; venture-specific complexity must remain isolated.
4. Every initiative has one accountable owner and explicit acceptance criteria.
5. Decisions are recorded, reviewable, and reversible where practical.
6. Agents advise and execute within delegated boundaries; they do not create their own authority.

## Company-OS v1 Objective

Establish a reliable, auditable, supervised agent operating model before optimizing for autonomy or adding external integrations.
